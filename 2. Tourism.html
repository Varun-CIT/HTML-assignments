Tourist Destinations

Paris, France

Paris, the City of Light, is famous for its stunning architecture, art museums, and romantic atmosphere.

Getting There:

By Air: Charles de Gaulle Airport (CDG) and Orly Airport (ORY)

By Train: TGV trains connect Paris to major European cities

Accommodation:

From budget-friendly hostels to luxury hotels, Paris has a wide range of options. Popular areas include Montmartre, Le Marais, and Champs-Élysées.

New York City, USA

The city that never sleeps, New York City is known for its iconic landmarks, diverse culture, and entertainment options.

Getting There:

By Air: John F. Kennedy International Airport (JFK), LaGuardia Airport (LGA), and Newark Liberty International Airport (EWR)

By Train: Amtrak trains connect NYC to major US cities

Accommodation:

From budget-friendly hostels to luxury hotels, NYC has a wide range of options. Popular areas include Midtown Manhattan, Greenwich Village, and Brooklyn.

Tokyo, Japan

Tokyo is a vibrant city that seamlessly blends traditional and modern culture, from ancient temples to cutting-edge technology.

Getting There:

By Air: Tokyo Narita International Airport (NRT) and Haneda Airport (HND)

By Train: Shinkansen bullet trains connect Tokyo to other major Japanese cities

Accommodation:

From budget-friendly guesthouses to luxury hotels, Tokyo has a wide range of options. Popular areas include Shinjuku, Shibuya, and Asakusa.
